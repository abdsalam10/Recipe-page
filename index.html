MAKING PASTA

Pasta means a type of Italian food made from a dough of flour and water (sometimes eggs), shaped into various forms, and cooked by boiling.

Homemade Fresh Pasta (Basic Dough)

Ingredients (2–3 servings)

  • 2 cups (240g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil (optional, for softer dough)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Extra flour for dusting

STEP IN MAKING PASTA

  1. Make a flour well
    • Pour the flour onto a clean working surface.
    • Make a deep well in the center.
    • Add the eggs, salt, and olive oil into the center.
  2. Mix the dough
    • Use a fork to gently beat the eggs while gradually pulling in flour from the edges.
    • When the mixture becomes thick, start using your hands to bring it together.
  3. Knead
    • Knead the dough for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • If sticky → sprinkle a little flour.
    • If dry → add a few drops of water.
  4. Rest the dough
    • Wrap the dough in plastic wrap.
    • Let it rest at room temperature for 20–30 minutes.
  5. Roll out the dough
    • Cut the dough into 2–4 pieces.
    • Using a pasta machine or rolling pin, roll each piece until thin (setting 5–6 on a machine).
